Research Directions

Efficient Temporal Consistency

Problem: High-fidelity video generation historically requires overwhelming compute clusters.

Approach: Investigating compact, expressive representations that decouple spatial and temporal rendering.

Impact: Aiming to democratize generative ML by making training highly accessible.

Multimodal Abstract Alignments

Problem: Standard models fail to align dense visual video cues with abstract language reasoning.

Approach: Exploring robust representation learning systems for deep cross-modal consistency.

Impact: Enabling deep video-understanding models capable of complex contextual inference.
